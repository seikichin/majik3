Slots can also be `abbreviated' with some bigger bodyparts:


leg        -    shin, boot
arm        -    bracer, hand
legs       -    leg, leg
arms       -    arm, arm
upper body -    torso, arms, belt
full body  -    head, torso, arms, legs
